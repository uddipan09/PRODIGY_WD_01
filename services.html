Services

    2. Dinner, snacks & breakfast
    3. Mattress with pillow, bed cover & blankets
    4. Toilet
    5. Fresh drinking water
    6. Hot water
    7. Table with seating arrangements
    8. Boating
    9. Boat safari for Dolphin watching
    10. All safety maritime equipments

  • 7. Elephant Safari: Come, embark on the enchanting journey of an elephant safari, where the gentle giants guide you through the untamed beauty of the wild, offering a unique and awe-inspiring perspective on nature's grandeur.
